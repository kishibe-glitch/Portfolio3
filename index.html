Development Process
Briefing

I work with the client to define the goals and objectives of the future website. We draw up detailed technical specifications. You provide all the content that is available.

Competitor Analysis

I gather all information about the business and conduct UX research. I work through objections. I look for references and decide on the style of the future website.

Prototype Development

I create a site map, think through the interaction, work out the sections, the sequence of blocks, and the texts. I draw a prototype of the main page of the site.

Design

I present the finished design of the main page of the site, present my vision, and justify my decisions. After approval, I finish the rest of the pages.
